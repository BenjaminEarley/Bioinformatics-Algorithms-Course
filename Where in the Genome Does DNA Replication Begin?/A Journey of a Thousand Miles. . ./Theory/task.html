Welcome to Bioinformatics Algorithms!

Thank you for joining us! This is the official text of the Bioinformatics Specialization on Coursera.

If you're on the go, you can find a print companion at the textbook website or download an e-book from LeanPub. These resources contain bonus content not featured in the interactive text in the form of "Detours" that didn't quite make the cut into the main text.

We say "read" because we have added lots of interactive features to make this book fun. As you progress, you will find STOP and Think questions helping you reflect on the material and Exercise Breaks testing your knowledge.

If you are following the Honors Track, then Code Challenges allow you to implement the bioinformatics algorithms that you encounter, You will also encounter Charging Station mini-lessons along your way that will guide you through tricky computational concepts. For best results, try solving a Code Challenge before proceeding to a Charging Station.

On the next step, you'll see the excellent work of Randall Christopher, who provided fun cover art for every chapter. This textbook also owes a debt to Son Pham and Glenn Tesler, who provided much valuable feedback on the manuscript.

Yet our most important critic is YOU. We have already made hundreds of changes to this text based on student feedback since 2013, and we plan to continue doing so.
